Aim:
The primary aim of this study is to examine how various branding strategies influence consumer purchase decisions within the online retail sector. As e-commerce continues to grow rapidly, understanding the branding techniques that effectively drive consumer engagement and conversion has become essential for online retailers.

Objectives:

To identify and categorize the most commonly adopted branding strategies by successful online retailers.

To evaluate consumer perceptions and attitudes toward these branding strategies through the lens of online shopping behavior.

To analyze the correlation between specific branding tactics—such as emotional branding, influencer marketing, and personalization—and actual consumer purchase behavior.

To provide actionable recommendations for e-commerce businesses seeking to strengthen brand-consumer relationships and enhance online sales performance.
